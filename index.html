About the Game

Dive into a dystopian world where the lines between humanity and madness blur. "Project ZERØTH" immerses you in a gripping RPG experience where your every decision shapes the story.

Gameplay Mechanics

Explore an expansive open world teeming with mysteries, dangers, and opportunities. Your choices dictate alliances, rivalries, and the fate of the world itself.

  • Immersive character progression with a deep skill tree.
  • Dynamic combat blending strategy and action.
  • Environmental storytelling interwoven with player-driven narratives.

The World of ZERØTH

Step into a realm inspired by '50s noir and dystopian futures. Explore sprawling cities, desolate wastelands, and secretive underground factions in a world steeped in atmosphere.

Key Features

  • Non-linear storytelling driven by your actions.
  • Richly detailed environments with hidden lore.
  • Complex characters and factional intrigue.
  • Atmospheric soundtracks that immerse you in the world.
