About the Game

Step into a dark and immersive open-world RPG where every choice matters. Explore a world ravaged by a mysterious virus, uncover secrets, and forge your path in a universe filled with danger and intrigue. Project ZERØTH is a passion-driven creation from a team of dreamers, offering a story like no other.

Gameplay Mechanics

Engage in an open-world environment with a non-linear narrative. Experience dynamic interactions with characters, factions, and the mysterious virus that reshapes humanity.

  • Character-driven storylines influenced by your choices.
  • Intense combat and stealth mechanics tailored to your playstyle.
  • Deep crafting systems to aid your survival in the world of ZERØTH.
