About the Game

Step into a dark and immersive open-world RPG where every choice matters. Explore a world ravaged by a mysterious virus, uncover secrets, and forge your path in a universe filled with danger and intrigue. Project ZERØTH is a passion-driven creation from a team of dreamers, offering a story like no other.

Key Features

  • Non-linear storytelling with player-driven choices.
  • Immersive open-world exploration.
  • Complex characters and factions to interact with.
  • A dark, atmospheric setting inspired by the '50s-'90s.
